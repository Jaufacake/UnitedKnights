Why Learn Chess?

  • Problem Solving

    It is well known that chess sparks creativity and encourages thinking outside the box - perfect to set you up for success.

  • Competition

    From competing locally to travelling the world, chess could be the game that complements your competitive spirit and drive for success.

  • Fun!

    The most important thing, is to find something you enjoy. Chess brings that joy from all around the world, so let your kids join in the fun!

Community

All of our chess clubs have a tight knit community. We run 2 yearly chess tournaments that receive over 100 participants, with events and camps in between that focus on improving your chess skills. Aside from this, all of our chess clubs include in-school tournaments during term time, so your child will have plenty of opportuniites to practice and play!

Events
